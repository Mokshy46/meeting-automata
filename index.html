Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glu.

How is Glu different from other meeting note tools?

Glu goes beyond simple note-taking by automating your entire meeting workflow. While other tools just transcribe, Glu extracts action items, creates tasks, and connects seamlessly to your favorite productivity tools, saving you hours of manual work after every meeting.

How does the automation feature work?

Glu uses advanced AI to understand the context of your meetings. It identifies action items, decisions, deadlines, and key information, then automatically routes this data to the right destinations in your workflow tools based on rules you set up.

Which tools and platforms does Glu integrate with?

Glu connects with most popular productivity suites, task management platforms, note-taking apps, and CRM systems. Our open API also allows for custom integrations with your team's unique workflow tools and internal systems.

Is my data secure and confidential?

Absolutely. Glu employs enterprise-grade encryption for all data, both in transit and at rest. We never share your data with third parties, and our systems are compliant with SOC 2 and GDPR requirements. Your business conversations remain completely confidential.

How accurate is the meeting transcription?

Glu's transcription technology achieves over 98% accuracy for clear audio. Our AI is trained on diverse business terminology and industry-specific jargon, making it highly effective for all types of meetings. The system also improves over time as it learns from your team's communication patterns.

How much time can Glu save my team?

Teams typically save 5-10 hours per week using Glu. This includes time previously spent taking notes during meetings, manually extracting action items, entering tasks into project management tools, updating CRMs, and creating follow-ups. Glu automates all these post-meeting workflows so your team can focus on the work that matters.
